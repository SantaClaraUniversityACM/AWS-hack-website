About Amazon


We're positive you've used Amazon before, whether to buy that last minute thing or because you don't want to leave the comfort of your own home, but have you used AWS? AWS is arguably THE largest cloud computing platform in the world with thousands of companies using their services. They offer a wide range of services from databases and storage to raw compute power, but they are offering YOU their latest, bleeding edge AI technology. Catch our AWS workshops with in person speakers to learn how to use their services!

About INRIX


INRIX is a global leader in mobility analytics and connected car services. We help cities and businesses use big data to identify and solve transportation problems, making the world safer, happier and greener. Our partners are automakers, governments, retailers, insurance agencies, advertisers and dozens of other industries that can benefit from understanding how people and vehicles move.

Prizes

3 prizes, 2 tracks, 1 hackathon

Main Track: AWS-Powered AI for Scalable Solutions

Create a full product that could be sold to companies with potential costs and benefits accounted for using AWS services. Must use AWS services in some way. all types of projects are encouraged!

1st Place: $1500 | 2nd Place: $500 | 3rd Place: $250 | Graduate: $500

Side Track: Enhance transportation safety, efficiency, and sustainability

Create a solution to a problem, air, land, and/or sea, that could say money, lives, or the environment. Use of AWS tools preferred but not required. More focused for teams that would prefer a starting topic

1st Place: $750 | 2nd Place: $500

Frequently Asked Questions

What's a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an opportunity when you and your team come together to construct a cool and inventive project in 24 hours!


How big can my team be?

We require all teams to be comprised of 2-6 people. This is a great way for you to work with others, and learn about the design process.

What if I don't have a team?

No problem! We will help connect you with other students who are also looking for teammates before and during the event.

Who can attend?

While this event is targeted toward CS/Coen majors, all Undergrad and Graduate students are welcome.

I've never been to a Hackathon!

No worries! We have a team of mentors to help you along the way. Previous project experience will be helpful.

Any other questions?

Feel free to email us at acm@scu.edu if you have any questions or concerns!
